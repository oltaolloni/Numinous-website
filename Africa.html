AFRICA

The greatest wildlife
spectacle on earth

Africa is the world’s premier wildlife watching destination. From coming face to face with a troop of gorillas in the jungle and feeling a million wildebeest hooves thunder across the Serengeti on their migration, to following big cats on the prowl through the bush and breathtaking encounters elephants on foot, there is nowhere like it.

And Africa is about so much more than safari. There are vibrant cities to explore with unique creative, culinary and cultural scenes. Mountains to hike and great waterfalls to marvel at. Beautiful stretches of coastline and idyllic islands where you can’t help but relax into the slower pace of life. And the real reason we love Africa – it's people.
